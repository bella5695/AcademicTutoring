How It Works?

The tutoring service works like this:

  • Step 1

    First, select a subject from the subject menu that you would like to be tutored on.

  • Step 2

    Then, go to the contact page, where you can fill out the form for your desired subject for tutoring.

  • Step 3

    After, you will get a zoom link to join the first tutoring session for the subject that you need guidance with. Payment is required.
